About Me

Hi! I am a bioinformatician with a BSc (Hons) in Biochemistry and an MRes in Big Data Biology, awarded with distinction and recognition on the Dean's List from the University of Southampton.

My expertise includes proficiency in Python and R, which I use to develop tools for data analysis, machine learning, and visualization. I also specialize in genomics, transcriptomics, deep learning, machine learning, and the development of computational pipelines, alongside strong skills in SQL/PostgreSQL.

I am passionate about leveraging data-driven approaches to answer important biological questions and solve complex research problems.

Investigating human breast tissue cohort heterogeneity and its association with Annexin A1 through multiplex imaging and deep learning

My MRes dissertation analysed human breast tissue to uncover heterogeneity in molecular subtypes and tumor grades in breast cancer using multiplex immunohistochemistry imaging and deep learning.

Cellpose Quantification

A Python based command-line interface tool developed to process multiplex immunohistochemistry images of cells alongside cell segmentation masks. The tool generates single-cell data by extracting and quantifying features from segmented regions, enabling downstream analysis of cellular phenotypes and marker expression patterns.
